# Core features

* Photo Capture 
   * Take a photo (P: iOS, J: android)
   * Store/Retrieve Photo
   * metadata on photo
   * geolocation
  
* Categorization of photos
   * sort / view by metadata
   * List view of photos 
   
* Sharing of photos
  * Authentication
  * Add / remove friends
  * Sharing of photos based on permissions
